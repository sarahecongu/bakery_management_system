

1. **Product Table**:
   - ProductID (Primary Key)
   - ProductName
   - CategoryID (Foreign Key)
   - Price
   - StockQuantity
   - Description

2. **Category Table**:
   - CategoryID (Primary Key)
   - CategoryName

3. **Order Table**:
   - OrderID (Primary Key)
   - CustomerID (Foreign Key)
   - OrderDate
   - TotalAmount
   - Status (e.g., Pending, Completed, Shipped)

4. **OrderDetails Table**:
   - OrderDetailID (Primary Key)
   - OrderID (Foreign Key)
   - ProductID (Foreign Key)
   - Quantity
   - UnitPrice
   - Subtotal

5. **Customer Table**:
   - CustomerID (Primary Key)
   - FirstName
   - LastName
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Address

6. **Employee Table**:
   - EmployeeID (Primary Key)
   - FirstName
   - LastName
   - Position
   - Salary
   - HireDate

7. **Vendor Table**:
   - VendorID (Primary Key)
   - VendorName
   - ContactName
   - Phone
   - Email
   - Address

8. **Feedback Table**:
       - CustomerID (Foreign Key)
   - FeedbackDate
   - FeedbackText

9. **Inventory Table**:
   - ProductID (Foreign Key)
   - Quantity
   - ExpiryDate

10. **Branch Table**:
   - BranchID (Primary Key)
   - BranchName
   - Location
   - ManagerID (Foreign Key)

11. **Promotion Table**:
    - PromotionID (Primary Key)
    - PromotionName
    - Description
    - DiscountPercentage

12. **Sales Table**:
    - SaleID (Primary Key)
    - ProductID (Foreign Key)
    - SaleDate
    - Quantity
    - UnitPrice
    - TotalAmount

13. **Payment Table**:
    - PaymentID (Primary Key)
    - OrderID (Foreign Key)
    - PaymentDate
    - PaymentMethod
    - Amount

14. **Schedule Table**:
    - ScheduleID (Primary Key)
    - EmployeeID (Foreign Key)
    - ShiftDate
    - StartTime
    - EndTime

15. **Stock Table**:
    - StockID (Primary Key)
    - ProductID (Foreign Key)
    - Quantity
    - ReorderLevel
